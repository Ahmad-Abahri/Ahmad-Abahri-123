The Meaning of colors

  • the black:

    • It means mourning for the oppression and arbitrary oppression that the people of Palestine suffer from, and it means the sadness and crying that rises over the faces of orphaned children who have lost their father

  • the white:

    • It means peace and love, and it is the message of the prophets who were sent to the beloved land of Palestine. It means that our hearts are white, do not bear hatred against anyone, and are only filled with love for others, unlike our enemies who know nothing but hatred and hatred

  • the Green:

    • The green lands and plains of Palestine, which means goodness, growth, blessing, and hope for the future. It means that the sprouts of Palestine will grow and grow to be one day green branches laden with fruits. It means that the barren land that the enemy burned will return once again to the joy of the new spring

  • the Red:

    • The color of blood means martyrdom, sacrifice and giving, and is it greater than a person obtaining the honor of martyrdom defending his religion, family and homeland?
